We care for you, We are sponsors of Pinkathon

Our commitment for better humanity starts with fitness of individual body and mind. At Aurospaces we not only develop great platforms but also focus on contributing back to society. Our founder himself being the cyclist (Member of the BBC, BBCh, and GoGreen Club) we evaluated various programs for sponsorship and also active participation. Zeroed on few considering the current issues and to bring the awareness and go towards leading a better life. Considering the current problem of breast cancer in India and to bring awareness via active participation we have chosen this to be our first Corporate Social Responsibility program. From the word go our directors already participated in Namma Bengaluru pinkathon and sponsored by meeting the founder of this awareness campaigner Mr. Milind Soman. We are really elated to be part of this and continue to support this campaign for the welfare ness of our great Indian Women.
